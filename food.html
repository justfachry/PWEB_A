FOOD My Hometown, Duri is has 2 Traditional foods that i know.

1. Bolu Kemojo

Bolu Kemojo is made from flour mixture that is given pandanus flavor and suji leaf aroma and cooked with unique mold. Then, it serve by divided into 8 pieces. I really like this spongecake because its unique texture and aroma.

2. Gulai Ikan Patin

Gulai ikan patin or catfish curry is the traditional food in Riau that usually served when have dinner with family. Catfish curry is also served in wedding party or cultural event. The main ingredient is catfish that cooked with curry soup. I rarely eat these foods because I don't really like fish.
